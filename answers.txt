
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git is a version control system that helps track changes in code, collabrate with others, and maintain a history of modifications. 
It prevents data loss, enables branching for feature development, and simplifies merging code across team members.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to various fields, including healthcare, finance, marketing, and cybersecurity.
For example, in healthcare, it is used to predict disease outbreaks and personalize treatment plans based on specific patient data.
